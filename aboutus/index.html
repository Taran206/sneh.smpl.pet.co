SMPL Principles
Our six non-negotiables
These aren’t brand values. They’re how we actually operate - every day, every product, every decision behind the scenes. No fluff. No filler. Just standards that put your dog’s health first.
We follow the science
We formulate with veterinary nutritionists, not marketers. Every ingredient we use is backed by research, included at an effective dose, and chosen for a specific health benefit. If it isn’t supported by evidence, it doesn’t make the cut.
We keep it clean and minimal
Your dog doesn’t need 30 ingredients to be healthy. They need the right ones. We use no more than eight ingredients per formula - each one whole-food, human-grade, and carefully selected to do its job properly.
We’re transparent, full stop
No sneaky blends. No vague claims. We show you exactly what’s in the pouch, why it’s there, and where it came from - because you deserve the full picture, not the polished version.
We make it part of your real life
Health doesn’t have to be hard. Our supplements are designed to be easy - easy to use, easy to love, and easy to stick with. Because real results come from simple routines done well.
We give back where it matters
A portion of every purchase supports rescue dogs and shelters across the UK and Ireland. We believe every dog deserves care, not just the lucky ones - and we’re here to help close that gap.
We care about the bigger picture
From sustainable ingredient sourcing to lower-waste packaging, we’re working to reduce our environmental impact with every step because a healthy pup should live on a healthy planet.

WHY SMPL EXISTS

The pet aisle is broken.

Most dog supplements are made to hit price points, not health outcomes. They’re bloated with filler, vague on benefits, and impossible to decode - leaving you guessing about what your dog actually needs.

Enter SMPL.

We make science-led supplements using no more than eight whole-food ingredients per blend. No junk, no jargon - just clean formulas that work, created in-house with veterinary nutritionists.

Less really is more.

Our formulas are stripped back to only what matters. That means proven ingredients at effective doses - nothing extra, nothing watered down. When every ingredient earns its place, small becomes mighty.

Made for modern dog life.

From multi-functional blends to mess-free formats, everything we create is designed to slot into real routines. Health doesn’t have to be hard - it just has to be SMPL.

A Note from Our Founders

Like most good stories, ours started with a dog.

She came into our lives with a wagging tail, a few quirks, and a sensitive stomach that nothing seemed to fix. We tried everything - from vet visits to expensive tablets with shiny promises. Nothing stuck.

That’s when we realised the real problem wasn’t her - it was the industry. Too many pet products are built around clever marketing instead of actual outcomes. We wanted something better. Something simpler.

So we made it. SMPL is our answer to an overcomplicated space. It's our way of putting health back in your hands - and joy back in theirs.

Thanks for being part of it.

The SMPL Team
